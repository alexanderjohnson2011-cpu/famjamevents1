The Space

A backyard built for big moments.

19,000 square feet of private outdoor space in Thousand Oaks, CA — serving Ventura County and greater LA. Pool, hot tub, sprawling lawn, sand pits, and room for everyone you love.

🏊
Pool + Hot Tub
Deep end for swimming plus a heated hot tub. Shallow tanning ledge (only 2 ft deep — perfect for toddlers and lounging). Stocked with extensive pool toys.
⛺
The Setup
Canopy coverage: one 10×20 and two 10×10 canopies (up to 20×20 shade/rain cover). Six 6-foot tables, 4 cocktail tables, and up to 40 foldable chairs.
🌿
Grass Lawn
Expansive lawn with two sand pit areas — cornhole set included. Perfect for lawn games while kids run free.
🛋️
Cabana & Lounge
Shaded cabana area for guests who want to relax out of the sun without missing the action.
💡
String Lighting + Propane Heaters
Full string light coverage for evening events plus propane heaters to keep the party going when temps drop.
🚗
Parking
Extensive street parking available — we've never had an issue, even for events of 80+ people.
🚾
Restrooms
Indoor restroom access inside the home throughout your event.
🐾
Pet & Kid Friendly
Mesh safety fence keeps kids and dogs safe by the pool. It can be fully removed if you prefer an open layout.
